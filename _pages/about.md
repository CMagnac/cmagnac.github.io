A Doctor of Pharmacy who explores life.

Health and earth can't be disconnected, they stick together.
I prefer taking time to observe and understand rather than interpreting without reflexion.

My eagerness to learn about human behaviours, their cultures and languages set me on a path from which there is no escape.

My unwillingness to yield is to constantly inquiring my knowledges.
I am a lifelong learner.

After finishing my Pharm.D studies at Paris University René Descartes, I signed my first contract in South America. I stayed there for a decade.

In 2019, I came back in Strasbourg.
The time has come to share a part of my work.

Opioids are used to relieve moderate to severe pain.

The first-line treatment of constipation due to opioid painkillers may fail. Naloxegol, also known by its commercial name Moventig, may be prescribed after standard laxatives are removed. Here's a summary of a prescription example:

The prescription is for the treatment of pain due to cancer. The treatment duration is 28 days for each drug.

* Durogesic&reg; 12µg/h (fentanyl): 1 patch every three days
* Oxynormoro&reg; 5mg (oxycodone): 1 tablet every 6 hours in case of pain
* Moventig&reg; 25mg (naloxegol): 1 tablet per day

Fentanyl is a **synthetic**, lipophilic phenylpiperidine opioid agonist with analgesic and anesthetic properties. Oxycodone is a **semi-synthetic**, morphine-like opioid alkaloid with analgesic activity.

Both drugs selectively bind to and activate the mu-receptor in the central nervous system (CNS), thereby **mimicking the effects** of endogenous opiates.

Pain is as much objective as it is subjective. To determine how much a patient is suffering, doctors use a scale from 0 to 10 to measure the severity of the pain and decide which pain medicine to use.

The scale grade are:

* 0 means no pain.
* 1 to 3 means mild pain.
* 4 to 6 means moderate pain.
* 7 to 10 means severe pain

Major analgesics can be classified by their **equianalgesic dose**.

INN (International Nonproprietary Names)

| INN | Dose |
| :---: | :----: |

| Codeine   | 180mg  |

| Buprenorphine   | 0.4mg |

| Oxycodone | 15mg |
| Methadone | 3mg  |

## Moventig&reg;

### 1. Presentation

* Form: tablets
* Dose: 12.5 and 25 mg
* Recommended dose: 25mg tablet per day

### 2. Therapeutic indication

Naloxegol is a second-line treatment for constipation due to opioid therapy.

### 3. Pharmacology

It is a peripheral mu-opioid-receptor antagonist that blocks the mu-opioid receptors in the gut and reduces the side effects of opioids

### 4. Side effects

* abdominal pain
* diarrhoea
* headache and flatulence
